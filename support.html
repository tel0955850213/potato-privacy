Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?

No. POTATO works fully offline and stores your projects on your Mac. Signing in with Google is optional — it only turns on cloud sync so your focus data follows you across devices.

Will I lose my data if I sign in?

No. Signing in merges your local data with the cloud (it keeps the higher total time for each project) and uploads anything that isn't backed up yet. Your local data is never erased.

How do I reopen the main window after closing it?

Click the POTATO icon in the Dock to bring the main window back. The floating timer keeps running in the corner during a focus session.

What does Premium include?

A one-time purchase unlocks unlimited projects, extra animation themes (such as the boba tea theme), on-device flow detection, and cloud sync. You can restore your purchase on a new Mac with the same Apple ID via “Restore Purchase”.

How is my privacy handled?

See our Privacy Policy. In short: local-first, no tracking, no ads, and we never sell your data.

常見問題

一定要登入嗎？

不用。POTATO 完全離線可用，專案存在你的 Mac 上。用 Google 登入是選擇性的，只是開啟雲端同步，讓資料跨裝置同步。

登入會弄丟資料嗎？

不會。登入會把本機資料與雲端合併（每個專案取總時間較大者），並上傳尚未備份的資料，本機資料絕不會被清除。

關掉主視窗後怎麼重新打開？

點 Dock 上的 POTATO 圖示即可重新叫出主視窗。專注期間，角落的浮動計時器會繼續運作。

Premium 包含什麼？

一次買斷解鎖無限專案、更多動畫主題（例如波霸紅茶）、本機心流偵測與雲端同步。換新 Mac 用同一個 Apple ID 按「回復購買」即可。
